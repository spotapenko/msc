Quick Look

Quick Look

at Modern Style Cabinets

MSC Ltd Corp. (DBA - doing business as "Modern Style Cabinets"), is a fast growing modern kitchen and bathroom cabinetry wholesaler on the East Coast of the United States, with a showroom and production facilities located in Warminster, PA. The company mainly specializes in providing professional B2B services on kitchen and bathroom planning, assembly, and installation, but is also offering a wide variety of accompanying products - kitchen, bathroom hardware, and lighting - all at a very attractive wholesale price.
Since early 2012, Modern Style Cabinets is importing high quality products from factories in Europe and Asia, and its warehouse is full of goods ready for assembly and/or immediate delivery to fulfill your business needs. The company offers flexible multi-tier pricing on all products to ensure that no one of its clients is left disappointed. Loyal customers receive additional benefits, including significant discounts on all our product lines, as well as on all related services provided by the company - assembly, packaging, delivery, and installation.
